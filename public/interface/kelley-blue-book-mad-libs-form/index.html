Kelley Blue Book Mad Libs Form

The Kelley Blue Book features Mad Libs-style inline form elements for its contact form. The contact form, which was previously displayed with right-aligned labels adjacent to to left aligned fields, is now displayed as an inline form with "fill in the blanks" style prompts for input.
