Deliverables

About

Deliverables are documents produced as part of the user experience design process.

Peter Morville and Jeffrey Callender compiled and summarized a list of UX deliverables on the Semantic Studios site.

Peter Boersma also gave a thorough presentation about the use of "UX Deliverables in Practice" at the IA Konferenz 2009.
