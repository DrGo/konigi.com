Icons for Interaction

This is the presentation from Jon Hicks’ @media 2009 Icons for Interation talk in London, which includes speaking notes for every slide. Hicks demonstrates effective design and strategies for use, providing plenty of examples, and a link list that points to the resources and tips mentioned. You can download the PDF of the talk.
