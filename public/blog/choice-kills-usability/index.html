Choice Kills Usability

John S. Rhodes offers a great reminder in this short article on Apogee of how to focus narrowly on singular goals to increase usability.

Key things to remember:

  • One of the easiest ways to improve usability is by focusing on just one thing.
  • Provide a clear vision. Be practical. Focus for usability.
